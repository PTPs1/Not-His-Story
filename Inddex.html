About Our Mission

                
                    

Not His Story, But Ours is dedicated to preserving, celebrating, and sharing the authentic narratives of Black experiences that have been overlooked or misrepresented in mainstream media and historical accounts.

                                         
                        
                            

Preserve

                            

We archive and digitize important cultural artifacts, oral histories, and documentary materials.

                        
                        
                            

Educate

                            

We create educational resources that highlight untold stories and perspectives.

                        
                        
                            

Empower

                            

We support new Black storytellers in sharing their vision and creative works.

Historical Timeline

                

Journey through significant moments in Black cinema and cultural history.

                                 
                    
                                                              
                        
                        
1915
                        
                            

The Birth of a Response

                            

In response to D.W. Griffith's racist film, Black filmmakers began creating their own narratives to counter harmful stereotypes and tell authentic stories.

                            Learn More                         
                    
                                                              
                        
                        
1920s
                        
                            

The Harlem Renaissance

                            

A cultural revival of African American arts, including literature, music, theater, and film, emerged during this period, creating new platforms for Black voices.

                            Learn More                         
                    
                                                              
                        
                        
1940s
                        
                            

Race Films

                            

Independent films featuring all-Black casts made for Black audiences flourished, creating an alternative cinema ecosystem outside of Hollywood's discriminatory system.

                            Learn More
